01 · What the RFP required
Three assessment dimensions
Compact summary of the formal RFP requirements (rev. 25 Mar 2026).
Compliance
Unified PDBI programme
  • Master Policy + local fronting
  • 11 minimum All Risks coverages
  • CAR Facility for greenfields (LEG 3 + ALOP)
  • Min. 2 loss adjusters per local policy
  • Min. 1 inspection per site during the contract
  • Reinsurance panel transparency with % share
  • Compliance with Brazilian Insurance Law 15.040/2024
Network
Coverage in 11 countries
Minimum: 3 senior professionals (1 per sub-region):
  • Southern Cone · BR/UY/PY · base SP or Curitiba
  • Andean · CL/CO/PE/EC · base Santiago or Bogotá
  • Mexico & Caribbean · MX/PA/GT/RD · base Monterrey or Mexico City
Tie-breaker: own offices or legal representatives in the 11 countries.
Cost
Hybrid compensation model
  • Brazil = fixed service fee + Success Fee Clause (no direct commission)
  • 10 countries = brokerage commission % embedded in policy (gross)
  • Success Fee = % of savings vs 2025 (broker-proposed)
  • Mandatory disclosure of reinsurance panel + reinsurance commission
